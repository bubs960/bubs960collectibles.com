Make it Right

Returns & Refunds

If the piece isn't what you expected, we make it right. Here's how.

Last updated: April 2026

The Short Version

If a piece isn't as described — wrong condition, missing accessory, damaged in transit, misidentified — send it back for a full refund including return shipping. No stocking fees, no restocking runaround.

If you change your mind on an accurately-described piece, we'll take it back within 30 days; buyer covers return shipping in that case.

Return Window

  • Damaged / not-as-described: 48 hours from delivery to report, 14 days to return
  • Change-of-mind: 30 days from delivery
  • Final sale: Live-show purchases, clearance-tagged items, and custom lots — these are final unless damaged or not-as-described

Condition Requirements

Returned pieces must arrive in the same condition they shipped in, in original packaging where applicable. For MOC/sealed pieces, this means the seal has not been broken. For loose pieces, nothing new has been added, subtracted, or damaged.

How to Start a Return

  1. Message us at Bubs960toys@gmail.com with your order number and reason.
  2. If it's damaged or not-as-described: include photos.
  3. We reply within 24 hours with return instructions and, when applicable, a prepaid label.
  4. Pack the piece back the way it came to you.
  5. Ship it. Refund processes within 3 business days of arrival.

Refund Method

Refunds return to the original payment method. Shopify processes most in 3–5 business days; your bank may take another day or two to post.

Shipping Costs

  • Not-as-described or damaged: we cover both ways
  • Change of mind: you cover return shipping
  • Original shipping fees are refundable only on not-as-described returns

Exchanges

We don't do direct exchanges — return for refund, then buy the replacement. It keeps the accounting clean and the process fast.

Lost Return Packages

Always ship returns with tracking. If a return goes missing in transit, we work with you and the carrier on a claim.

Questions?

If any of this feels off for your situation, message us. We read every return request and handle each one human-to-human: Bubs960toys@gmail.com.
