Vintage Action Figure
Value Guide

What your 80s and 90s action figures are worth right now. All numbers from real eBay sold comps — not wishful asking prices.

Sitting on a box from your childhood bedroom? Cleaning out a parent's attic? This is the current 2026 market for the big vintage action-figure lines. Values are based on completed eBay listings over the last 60 days, segmented by condition. For a custom quote on your specific pieces, send photos and we'll come back in 24 hours.

G1 Transformers (1984-1990)

Still the king of vintage lines. G1 has gone up consistently for 15 years and 2026 shows no slowdown. Rarer characters (Fortress Maximus, Omega Supreme, Predaking full set) command wild premiums. Condition is everything — chrome rub and sticker condition drive 2x-5x swings.

Vintage Star Wars (Kenner, 1977-1985)

The blue-chip of vintage collecting. AFA-graded MOC pieces sell for serious money at the high end. Loose figures are more accessible but the "complete with accessories" requirement is strict — a missing lightsaber or weapon tanks value 40%+.

G.I. Joe: A Real American Hero (1982-1994)

Huge active collector base. "Swivel-arm battle grip" figures (1983+) are the sweet spot. File cards and accessories matter — Joes without their gear lose half their value. Large vehicles and HQ playsets command their own premium.

What's Hot and What's Cooling in 2026

🔥 Hot Right Now

  • G1 Transformers combiners (Devastator, Predaking)
  • MOC vintage Star Wars from original trilogy
  • Sealed cases of any vintage line
  • LJN figures with original boxes
  • Kenner Batman / Super Powers complete with capes
  • G1 Headmasters and Targetmasters

❄️ Softer This Year

  • Loose 90s Marvel / Toy Biz (market saturated)
  • Common-wave MOTU without playsets
  • Late-series Hasbro WWF (overcooked market)
  • Power Rangers (peaked in 2021-22)
  • Common Joes without file cards
