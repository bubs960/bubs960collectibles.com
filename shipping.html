How We Ship

Shipping Policy

Fast, tracked, packed like the piece is going to our own shelf. Here's exactly how it works.

Last updated: April 2026

Processing Time

Orders ship within 1 business day of cleared payment, usually same day if placed before 3pm ET. You'll get tracking the moment the label prints.

Domestic Shipping (US)

We ship via USPS Ground Advantage (3–5 business days) as the default, with USPS Priority and UPS Ground available for larger or higher-value pieces.

  • Standard (under $100): flat rate, calculated at checkout
  • Priority / expedited: upgrade available at checkout or on request
  • Orders over $200: free Priority shipping (US only)
  • Sealed cases / oversized lots: quoted separately

International Shipping

We ship internationally to most countries via USPS First-Class Package International or Priority Mail International. Rates vary by destination and weight — we quote before charging.

International buyers are responsible for any customs duties, VAT, or import fees assessed by your country. We accurately declare contents and value — we don't mark parcels as "gift" or under-value them.

Packing Standards

Every piece is wrapped and packed the way we'd want something shipped to us:

  • Bubble wrap on every item, minimum
  • Double-boxed for MOC pieces, sealed cases, and high-value items
  • Corner protectors on every carded figure
  • Void fill so nothing shifts in transit
  • Rigid mailers for graded slabs

Tracking

Every shipment includes tracking. The number is emailed the second it's generated and also available in your order confirmation. Questions about a package in transit? Reply to the shipping notification or message us directly.

Damaged in Transit

If a piece arrives damaged, message us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the package and the damage. Every shipment is insured — we work with you on a full refund, partial refund, or replacement depending on what fits. We've never not made it right.

Lost Packages

If tracking stalls for more than 10 business days (US) or 21 days (international), reach out. We'll open a claim with the carrier and either refund or reship.

Local Pickup / Drop-off

Local to the Boston area? Pickup can be arranged on larger purchases or collection buys — message to coordinate.

Questions?

Shipping is the difference between a good seller and a great one. If anything feels off, reach out: Bubs960toys@gmail.com.
