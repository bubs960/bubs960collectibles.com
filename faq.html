FAQ

The questions I get most — answered straight. Don't see yours? Shoot me a message, I read every one.

Authenticity & Condition

Are all pieces 100% authentic?

Yes. Every figure is hand-inspected before it's listed. I don't sell knock-offs, reproductions, or "display" fakes. If something's a reissue or a variant, it's labeled as such in the title or description — no surprises.

How do you describe condition?

The way I'd want it described if I were buying: honest, specific, and with flaws called out before you see them.

Quick guide:

MOC / Sealed: Mint on card or factory-sealed. Any card wear, bubble lift, or sticker residue is noted.
Loose — Complete: Out of the package with all original accessories.
Loose — Incomplete: Missing one or more accessories (listed specifically).
Played With: Joint wear, paint rubs, or minor flaws. Photographed up close.

Can I get more photos before buying?

Always. Hit the contact form with the figure you're looking at and what you want to see — joints, backside of the card, specific paint detail, whatever. I'll send fresh shots within a day.

Shipping & Packaging

How do you pack figures?

Bubble wrap on every piece, double-boxed when the figure warrants it (MOC pieces, grails, anything fragile), and corner protection on cards. My rule: pack it like it's going to my shelf.

How fast do you ship?

Usually same-day or next-day after cleared payment. Tracking goes out the moment the label prints. You'll know where your figure is from "scanned" to "delivered."

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — message first for a quote. International shipping rates vary a lot by country and weight, and I'd rather give you the real number than a guess. Duties and customs fees are the buyer's responsibility.

What if it arrives damaged?

Message me immediately with photos of the package and the figure. I'll work with you on a refund, partial, or replacement — whatever makes it right. Every shipment is insured.

Buying, Trading, Returns

Can I trade instead of buying?

Yes — I love trades. Figures for figures, grails for grails. Message me with what you have and what you're eyeing. I'd rather move a piece to the right shelf than squeeze a few extra bucks. If you have a whole collection instead of a single trade, see We Buy Collections — same fair-deal mindset applied to bulk.

Do you take offers?

Reasonable ones, yes. Lowballs get a polite pass. Bundle offers (multiple pieces at once) tend to move best — I'll almost always cut a deal on a multi-piece pickup.

What's your return policy?

If the piece isn't as described, send it back — full refund on arrival. Accurately-described pieces are final sale; we don't take change-of-mind returns. Read the listing, ask for more photos before buying, and we'll make sure you know exactly what's coming.

Full refund/return terms will be auto-generated by Shopify at launch and linked here.

Can I put a figure on hold?

Short holds (24–48 hours) for serious buyers — just message. No multi-week holds. Want-list tracking is free and open-ended: tell me what you're hunting and I'll ping you when I get one.

I'm hunting a specific figure — can you find it?

Probably. Send me the want list (be as specific as possible — wave, variant, condition preferences) and I'll keep an eye out. No fees for hunting, no obligation to buy if I find it.
