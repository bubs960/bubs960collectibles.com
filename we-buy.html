Two Ways to Sell

Pick the path that fits. Either way, the quote is fair and the money comes fast.

Buy Outright

Cash. Today.

We pay you a flat lump sum, we take the collection, you're done. No waiting, no back-end drama, no "we'll send you a check when it sells."

  • Best for: estates, big piles, fast-moving pieces, when you just want it gone
  • Timeline: quote → agree → payment within 24 hours of inspection
  • Payment: PayPal, Venmo, Zelle, or cash in hand
Consign

Sell For More, Together.

We list, photograph, and move your piece on our storefront, eBay, and Whatnot. Our cut is a flat %, the rest is yours. You get full market value without doing any of the work.

  • Best for: high-value grails, slabbed pieces, anything where retail price matters
  • Timeline: 2–8 weeks depending on rarity (faster than you'd think — we know what moves)
  • Payment: you get paid the moment it sells, net of our consignment %

Quotes Backed by Real Comps

Every quote we give — whether we're buying outright or proposing a consignment price — starts from actual eBay sold listings, not wish-list asking prices. We look at what the piece has moved for in the last 30/60/90 days, across conditions, to set a number that's both fair to you and realistic for the market.

That's also why our stuff sells fast. We know what wave is hot, what's sitting, what's a sleeper — and we price accordingly.
