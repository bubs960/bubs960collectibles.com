How We Price — Read First

Before you submit. This sets realistic expectations and saves everyone time.

We're Collectors Running a Business

To offer what we do — fair quotes in 24 hours, no listing grind, both cash and consignment paths — we have to buy at a margin. We are not paying retail. If you need retail dollar for every piece, list them yourself on eBay — that's a totally valid choice.

The Math on a Buy-Outright Offer

Our typical cash-today offer lands around 40-60% of the eBay sold-comp average for your pieces. That % covers what a reseller eats that a private seller doesn't:

  • Platform fees — eBay / Whatnot take 13-15% when we turn around and sell
  • Payment processing — another 2-3%
  • Inventory holding — some pieces sit 6-12 months before the right buyer shows up
  • Shipping materials + labor — bubble wrap, boxes, corner protectors, labels, packing time
  • Risk — returns, damage, mis-grades, the occasional chargeback
  • Your time — the hours you skip photographing, listing, writing descriptions, answering buyer DMs

Consignment — You Get More

On consignment we list, photograph, market, and ship your pieces. You get back 65-80% of the eBay sold-comp after our commission. Trade-off: longer timeline — you get paid when the piece sells, not today.

Same $100 piece, sold on consignment$100
Our commission (typically 25%)≈ −$25
What you net$70 – $80

What This Means For You

  • Expect our outright quote on a $100 eBay piece to land around $40-$60
  • Expect a consignment payout of around $70-$80 (whenever it sells)
  • Rare grails, sealed cases, and case-fresh pieces get higher %
  • Bulk common pieces (common-wave Elites, loose modern figures) get lower %
  • If you're hoping for 90%+ of retail in cash, we're not your path — list it yourself

Still a Fair Deal?

Depends on your math. Here's what you're trading:

  • Time saved (no listings, no photos, no buyer messages, no shipping)
  • Zero platform fees or chargeback risk on your end
  • Cash in 24 hours (outright) or guaranteed market visibility (consignment)
  • One conversation vs fifty

For some sellers that's worth it. For others it's not. We'd rather you know going in than be disappointed at the quote.

Two Ways to Sell

Pick the path that fits. Either way, the quote is fair and the money comes fast.

Buy Outright

Cash. Today.

We pay you a flat lump sum, we take the collection, you're done. No waiting, no back-end drama, no "we'll send you a check when it sells."

  • Best for: estates, big piles, fast-moving pieces, when you just want it gone
  • Timeline: quote → agree → payment within 24 hours of inspection
  • Payment: PayPal, Venmo, Zelle, or cash in hand
Consign

Sell For More, Together.

We list, photograph, and move your piece on our storefront, eBay, and Whatnot. Our cut is a flat %, the rest is yours. You get full market value without doing any of the work.

  • Best for: high-value grails, slabbed pieces, anything where retail price matters
  • Timeline: 2–8 weeks depending on rarity (faster than you'd think — we know what moves)
  • Payment: you get paid the moment it sells, net of our consignment %

Quotes Backed by Real Comps

Every quote we give — whether we're buying outright or proposing a consignment price — starts from actual eBay sold listings, not wish-list asking prices. We look at what the piece has moved for in the last 30/60/90 days, across conditions, to set a number that's both fair to you and realistic for the market.

That's also why our stuff sells fast. We know what wave is hot, what's sitting, what's a sleeper — and we price accordingly.
