Meet Bubs960

Lifelong collector. Recovering auction-platform addict. The guy who knows which wave Mattel quietly dropped the exact figure you've been hunting for six months — and can probably get you one by next Thursday.

The Story

How we got here — short version.

It started the way every good collection does: a kid, an action figure, a toy aisle, and the feeling that the plastic in the bubble mattered more than whatever was on cable that night. Twenty-some years later that kid is me, the collection is bigger than it has any business being, and the want-lists still haven't shrunk.

Somewhere along the way the fun part of collecting got squeezed by the platforms. Thirteen percent here. A "promoted listing" there. Shipping gouge. Return fraud. I watched great pieces get priced for the fee structure instead of priced for collectors. That's a miserable way to move grails.

So this is the plan: cut the middleman, keep the photos honest, pack the mailers like the figures are going to my own shelf, and pass the savings to the people who actually care about which variant of Stone Cold has the unbroken vest snaps. If that's you — welcome. Pull up a ringside seat.
